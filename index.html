Solar-Powered Induction Cooktop

App. No. 202411104797

Induction cooktop powered directly from solar DC, bypassing the DC→AC→DC inverter chain. Uses a high-frequency H-Bridge circuit tuned to the coil's resonant frequency (20–50 kHz), maximising efficiency η.
